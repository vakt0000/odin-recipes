Description

A milkshake (sometimes simply called a shake) is a sweet drink made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, fruit syrup, or whole fruit into a thick, sweet, cold mixture.

The saving grace for milkshakes is the protein and calcium content. Since milkshakes contain ice cream and milk, which contains protein and calcium, you can expect to consume a good 15% of your daily protein and one-fifth of your daily calcium.
